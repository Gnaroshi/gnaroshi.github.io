# Hero Candidate Prompts

All candidates are private review artifacts under `artifacts/media-review/hero/`. They are true raster images, normalized to 2000x1600 (5:4), and must not be copied into `public/` before approval.

## Shared Constraints

Use case: `stylized-concept`

Asset type: personal research homepage Hero candidate

Composition: 5:4 landscape, two to four major visual elements, clear focal point, substantial right-side composition, crop-safe center, no important detail at the outer 12 percent.

Avoid: text, logo, readable code, interface, people, portrait, robot, robot face, glowing brain, floating UI, vector look, infographic, miniature desk, office desk, fake equipment, fake chart, fake screen, clay, plastic toy, fantasy armor, weapon, neon, cyberpunk, extreme gradient, watermark, excessive props, meaningless empty space.

## Review Rules

- Reject any candidate containing text-like marks, fake diagrams, interfaces, or human/robot/character cues.
- Reject if the focal point disappears in the mobile crop shown at `/dev/media-review/hero/`.
- Reject if the image reads as a product mockup, furniture arrangement, or generic AI still life.
- Approval must identify exactly one candidate ID or explicitly request another iteration.
